The data measure the buildings that the ischool has purchased since 1700. In my plot, the year that the building is constructed is on x axis and year acquired is on the y axis. The mark I used for the graph is circle, because I want to show how many buildings are constructed and acquired at each time slot. Using the circle can make the plot easy to see because if many buildings are constructed and acquired at the same time set, the color of the circle will get darker because they lay on top of each other.
